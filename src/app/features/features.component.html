Frejun's features and benefits are explained in detail for users

Take ownership of your meetings

The easiest meeting recordings, ever.

Sick and tired of fumbling around with the voice recorder on your phone when you’ve got an important meeting? Use the dial-in number or send a calendar invitation to meetings@frejun.com to record your meetings, and you’ll never look back.

Zero-fuss implementation.

With FreJun, there’s no need to bother with standalone apps or browsers. Simply invite FreJun to your call, and the tool will automatically dial in.

Streamline and optimize your workflow.

We don’t just log your meeting details; we also give you the power to add summaries, action items, and more to each Meeting within your dashboard. This streamlines your workflow, and allows team members to collaborate more effectively.

All your information, in one place.

FreJun isn’t just a tool that you can use to record meetings; it’s also a project management platform, a calendar, and a transcription tool. With all your information in one place, it’s easy to get things done.

Unlimited Usage

Want to export your meeting notes? It couldn’t get any easier. With a click of a button, you can send your data to Jira, Basecamp, Asana, Trello, Podio, and other collaborative tools.

Unlimited Meetings

Regardless of which plan you're on, you'll be able to record and store an unlimited number of meetings. Don't worry, we will never cut you off.

Everything you need for your company to grow

FreJun is built to help managers to keep track of their team members’ performance easily. With FreJun, you can get updates, assign to-dos, and keep an eye on your team’s progress, all in one place.

Say goodbye to multiple billings and invoices that are hard to track. FreJun offers unified billing, so your team members can be billed in a single card.
