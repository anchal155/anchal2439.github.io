Content Guidelines

  • Include at least 2-3 images for the content.
  • Include title, headings, and subheadings in the content.
  • Divide the content into small paragraphs for ease of readability.
  • Keep the sentences short with a maximum of 15 words per sentence.
  • If you are including facts and numbers in the article, research it thoroughly. Include the source along with the facts.
  • Try to write the articles in an active voice. Passive voice sentences should be kept at a maximum of 15%.
  • Edit your content well and check for grammatical and spelling errors before submitting it.
  • Write with a keyword in mind. Include the keyword in the title and first sentence of the blog. However, do not overstuff your content with keywords.
  • Along with information, you can add your experiences and advice related to the topic. That makes the content engaging.
  • Add at least 2 links within the post. The links should be related to the topic you're writing on. You can link to our previous blog posts too. We will love that!
  • Add your name, email, website, and social media handles at the bottom of the post.
  • Include a 2-3 sentence bio.
  • Please ensure that there is no plagiarism in your content. We have zero-tolerance for plagiarism and will strictly reject those content.

Some Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I submit through Google Docs?

Unlike Word, Google Docs makes it easy for us to edit and work on the document together as a team.

Should I pitch my topic before writing the post?

Yes, you can definitely do that, but it's not a necessity.

Can I include links to my posts?

Yes, if the links are relevant, then you can do so. Include at least 2 related links in your content. They could be links to your posts, other blogs, or our previous posts.

What happens if I do not follow the content guidelines?

The primary purpose of the guidelines mentioned above is to make your content readable and engaging. While we may edit some areas of your content for clarity, we advise you to follow the instructions thoroughly.
Not doing so will have your content rejected.

Will my post get dofollow links?

No, our guest posts do not get dofollow links. If you want dofollow links, you can email us anytime @ hello@frejun.com.

How long will it take for my post to get published?

We try to publish as soon as the assessment of the post has been done. We'll notify you once the post has been published.

What should I do after submitting my post?

We will notify you once your post is live. Engage in the comments and let your readers know that you're available for discussions. You can also share the post on social media platforms.

Is there any word limit for the submissions?

No, there isn't any word limit for the content. Just make sure that your post is not too short, i.e., below 300 words.
